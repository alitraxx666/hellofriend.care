How it works

Simple support, built for real life.

HelloFriend combines voice guidance, visual reminders, and caregiver connection into one simple system that helps people stay safe, supported, and independent.

🔔

Reminds

Voice reminders for medications, meals, and daily tasks such as charging a phone, locking doors, or replacing hearing aid batteries.

🧭

Guides

Clear visual instructions help users know exactly what to do — from the right pill compartment to the next step in their routine.

🤝

Connects

Keeps caregivers informed through confirmation, snooze logic, and future notification tools designed to support everyday independence.

Built for everyday independence

HelloFriend is designed for seniors, caregivers, and eventually other adults who may need extra help with routines, reminders, and daily living support.

  • Medication reminders
  • Meal and nutrition reminders
  • Daily task reminders
  • Caregiver support and escalation

First application: grandma.care

grandma.care is the first product powered by HelloFriend — inspired by a real family need and designed to help seniors and caregivers navigate everyday life with more clarity, safety, and confidence.

A simpler, safer way to stay on track.

We’re building technology that feels human, helpful, and easy to use — for caregivers, families, and the people they support.
